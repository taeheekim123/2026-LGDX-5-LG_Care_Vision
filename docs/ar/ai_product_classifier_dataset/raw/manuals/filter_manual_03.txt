Air-Conditioner Filter] How to clean and replace air-conditioner filter Troubleshooting Air Conditioners 10/27/2025 Print Email Copy Link Share Try this How to check and manage filter conditions ➔ Check the filters of the respective products. Filters with additional functions such as deodorization filter and TVF [total virus filter] may vary of applicability among models. Some filters are not allowed of washing with water, and required of replacement in regular cycle. Note Filters are consumables, and should be replaced with new filters when lifecycles of them are exhausted even in warranty period. Go to [Parts &amp; Accessories] on the customer service center in your area, or the LG Electronics website. Turn off the power, and unplug the power cord before cleaning the air-conditioner. Cleaning air-conditioner filter How to remove filters of wall-mounted air-conditioners varies depending on the front panel assembling types. ➔ Front position of panel [front position of filter] 1. Push the front panel up, and remove the ultra-fine filter. 2. Clean the filter with a vacuum cleaner, or a soft brush. 3. If it is severely contaminated, wash it in water using neutral detergent. 4. Leave the filter at a well ventilated shady place free from direct light to remove moisture, and then reassemble the filter. ➔ Top position of panel [top position of filter]: Filter on the top air intake vent 1. Push the top panel up, and remove the ultra-fine filter. 2. Clean the filter with a vacuum cleaner, or a soft brush. 3. If it is severely contaminated, wash it in water using neutral detergent. 4. Leave the filter at a well ventilated shady place free from direct light to remove moisture, and then reassemble the filter. Note Filters with additional functions such as deodorization filter and TVF [total virus filter] may vary in applicability among models. Some filters are not allowed of washing with water, and required of replacement in regular cycle. This guide was created for all models, so the images or content may be different from your product.
